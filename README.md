## 🔬 Research & Featured Projects (English)

### 🧠 [Project] GraphAlign (v8.0_graph) — Subject Interaction Graph for EEG-to-LLM Alignment
- **Status:** Active Development (Current Baseline: `v8.0_graph`)
- **Core Architecture:** An end-to-end multi-modal graph-enhanced framework aligning non-stationary continuous EEG signals directly into frozen Llama-3 4096-dimensional hidden semantic embeddings.
- **Technical Highlights:** 
  * Deployed a **Subject Interaction Graph (SIG)** backbone within the EEG Encoder to execute batch-wise topology inference, converting implicit subject identities into explicit topological coordinates.
  * Introduced **Topology-Gated Fusion** via a gated residual bridge to inject global multi-subject clustering priors into sovereign out-of-distribution (OOD) domains (`loso_ZPH`).
  * **The Breakthrough:** Successfully shattered the 0% zero-shot generalization barrier, achieving a monumental leap to **Unseen Top-5: 80.15%** and **Unseen Top-1: 7.63%**, while strictly preserving source domain safety (**Seen Top-5: 84.13% vs Peak 85.53%**).

### 📝 [Survey Paper] Vision-Language Models for Automated Reward Annotation in Embodied AI
- **Role:** Lead Author / Core Researcher
- **Status:** **Accepted for Publication** | **Invited for Oral Presentation & Poster Session** at International Conference (CONF-SEML 2026)
- **Contributions:** Established a fine-grained taxonomy for automated cross-modal reward annotation systems. Critically evaluated structural bottlenecks including *Reward Hacking* and *Visual Grounding* across multiple state-of-the-art embodied frameworks.

## 🔬 科研与核心项目 (中文)

### 🧠 [项目] GraphAlign (v8.0_graph) — 基于受试者交互图的脑电–大模型流形对齐框架
- **当前状态:** 持续高频迭代 (当前开源版本: `v8.0_graph`)
- **核心架构:** 主导开发了将原生非平稳 EEG 序列直接对齐至 frozen Llama-3 4096维隐藏语义空间的端到端深度图流形框架。
- **技术亮点:** 
  * 在 EEG 编码器内部署**受试者交互图（SIG）骨干网络**进行批次拓扑推断，将隐式的个体身份特征转化为显式的拓扑坐标系。
  * 引入**拓扑门控融合（Topology-Gated Fusion）**机制，通过门控残差桥为离群主体域（`loso_ZPH`）强制注入全局多受试者聚类先验。
  * **硬核战绩:** 彻底攻克了离群域“表征剥夺”的底层瓶颈，将原本锁死在 0% 的 **Unseen Top-5 检索准确率史诗级推至 80.15%**（Unseen Top-1 达 **7.63%**），且完全兼容 CLIP 风格的 InfoNCE 对抗训练模式，完美保障源域安全性（84.13%）。

### 📝 [学术综述] Vision-Language Models for Automated Reward Annotation in Embodied AI
- **角色:** 第一作者 / 核心研究员
- **状态:** **已被 CONF-SEML (2026) 正式录用** | 获邀进行大会**口头报告（Oral Presentation）**与海报展示
- **核心贡献:** 构建了具身智能自动化奖励标注的系统性指标分类图谱，深度剖析了多模态 Agent 落地中 *Reward Hacking* 与 *Visual Grounding* 的底层痛点。
